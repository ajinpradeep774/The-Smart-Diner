About us



Ever had to wait for a table at a restaurant for too long? Or had to drive in an endless loop around the locality to park your car before dining out? The common problem of a diner is the unavailability of seats in a restaurant or the unavailability of parking slots. If one or both of these are unavailable, then the customer will have to look for another place to dine, making the whole process of finding an ideal place to dine out very cumbersome. Here’s a smart idea. The Smart Diner app is the solution to this menacing problem. This app gives you a graphical layout of the restaurant and shows the status of a table, whether it is available or occupied. If available, the customer can reserve the seats and a parking slot, subject to a timeline within which the customer should occupy the reserved seats. These real time changes get updated on the web interface at the restaurant. Each restaurant owner can register and provide their own graphical layout of the restaurant and the parking slot. The user must log in with a username or mobile number and password so that the identity of the diner is known.
